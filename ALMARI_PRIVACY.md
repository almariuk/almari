# Almari — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**
**Governing law: England and Wales**

---

## Who we are

Almari is a peer-to-peer community marketplace for Indian ethnic wear in the UK diaspora, operated by Almari (contact: reachalmari@gmail.com, domain: almari.uk).

When this policy says "we", "us", or "Almari" it means the Almari platform and its operator.

---

## What data we collect and why

We collect only what we need to run the marketplace. Nothing more.

### When you register
- Your email address — to verify your account and send you transactional notifications
- A password — stored securely, never readable by us (handled by Supabase Auth)

### When you complete your profile
- Your first name and last initial — shown to other members on listings and transactions. Your full surname is never stored or shown.
- Your body measurements (bust, waist, hips, height, UK shoe size) — used only to sort listings by fit for you. **Only you can see your measurements.** They are never shown to other members, never used for advertising, never sold.

### When you add payment details (sellers only)
- Your payment details — the method you use to receive payment from buyers (for example, your PayPal handle, Revolut tag, or bank account details). This information is stored securely and shared only with buyers who have placed a confirmed order with you. It is never shown publicly on your profile or in listings.

### When you list an item
- Photos of the item — stored and displayed on your listing
- Item details — category, condition, colour, measurements, provenance (city, year, original price)
- Your reason for selling — a private motivation (never shown publicly) and a public phrase you select from a curated list

### When you buy or sell
- Transaction details — what was bought, for how much, when, by whom
- Your payment reference (ALM-XXXXX) — generated by Almari and used to match your payment to your order
- Postage and tracking information entered by the seller

### Automatically
- Basic usage data — which screens you visit, how the app performs. Used only to fix bugs and improve the experience. No advertising profiles built.

---

## What we do not collect

- Your full surname (first name and last initial only)
- Your home address (postage address is managed between buyer and seller; it is not stored by Almari)
- Your phone number
- Your social media profiles
- Any data about your ethnicity, religion, or background
- Location data or GPS tracking
- Card numbers or banking credentials — payments are made directly between buyer and seller; Almari does not process or handle payment transactions

## How we use your data

| Data | Why we use it |
|---|---|
| Email | Account verification, transactional notifications (sale, dispatch, delivery) |
| Name | Shown to other members in transactions |
| Measurements | Sort listings by fit for you — no other use |
| Payment details | Shared with buyers who place a confirmed order — no other use |
| Listing content | Display your listing to other members |
| Transaction history | Run the marketplace, resolve disputes, generate provenance certificates |
| Usage data | Fix bugs, improve the app |

**We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described below.**

---

## Who we share data with

We use a small number of trusted services to run Almari:

| Service | What they receive | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | All platform data (stored in their EU infrastructure) | Database and authentication |
| **Expo / Apple / Google** | Push notification tokens | Sending you app notifications |

Each of these services processes data only as instructed by us, under their own data processing agreements. We do not sell data to any of them or allow them to use it for their own purposes.

Payment details (sellers' PayPal handles, Revolut tags, or bank information) are shared directly with buyers who place a confirmed order. This sharing happens within the Almari platform and is limited to that transaction.

---

## Your measurements are sensitive data

Body measurements are personal. We treat them accordingly:

- They are stored securely in your account
- Only you can see them
- They are used for one purpose only: sorting listings by fit
- They are deleted when you delete your account
- They are never shared, never sold, never used for any other purpose

---

## How long we keep your data

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account data (name, email) | Until you delete your account |
| Measurements | Until you delete your account |
| Payment details | Until you remove them or delete your account |
| Listing data | Kept after account deletion — anonymised, not linked to you |
| Transaction records | Kept for 7 years (legal requirement for financial records) — anonymised after account deletion |
| Usage data | 90 days |

When you delete your account, your personal details are removed. Your listings and transaction records are kept in anonymised form to preserve the provenance chain for other members who were party to those transactions.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** — ask us what personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** — ask us to correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your account and personal data
- **Portability** — ask for a copy of your data in a readable format
- **Object** — object to how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at reachalmari@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have handled your data incorrectly.
